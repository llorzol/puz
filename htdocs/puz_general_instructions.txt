 <p>
  <b>Site</b>: Enter a location using the <img src="css/icons/search.png">search tool on the map or click anywhere 
  on the map within the Portland, Oregon metropolitan area (enclosed by the blue line of 
  the study boundary) to obtain estimates of the average depth to ground water, water-table elevation, land-surface elevation, and the uncertainty of the values for the selected location.
 </p>
 <p>
  The point representing the selected location will appear as a red dot. </br>
  <span><img  height="200" width="200" src="images/site_point_location.png" alt="Site point location" class="img-fluid"></span>
</p>
<p>
Once the scale is at a zoom level of twelve or more, a square box will appear instead of a red dot. The shading of the box
represents depth with values increase from light to dark purple. The box is 250 feet on each side and represents the 
maximum level of detail for the groundwater depth mapping. </br> 
  <span><img height="200" width="200" src="images/site_cell_location.png" alt="Site cell location"></span>
 </p>
 <p>
  To activate a popup message either hover over the red dot or the purple-shaded box to display the estimates 
  of the average depth to ground water, water-table elevation, land-surface elevation, and the uncertainty of 
  the values for the selected location. Additional warnings will be displayed 
  if the site is located outside of the modeled area or outside of the study area boundaries. 
 </p>
 <p>
  <b>Relative uncertainty for depth to groundwater</b>:
  <ul class="list-group list-group-flush">
  <li class="list-group-item">Low: 0 - 0.33</li>
  <li class="list-group-item">Medium: 0.34 - 0.67</li>
  <li class="list-group-item">High: 0.68 - 1</li>
</ul>
 </p>
